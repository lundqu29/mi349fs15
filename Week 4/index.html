So You Wanna Be A Youtuber?

A guide to finding your place within the YouTube Community!

By Jessica Lundquist

SoYouWannaBeAYoutuber.com will be directed toward those interested in being YouTube content creators. These past few years YouTube has become a growing media industry. But unlike past industries, anyone can be a creator. Even though anyone has the ability to upload to YouTube, being a successful Youtuber does not come easily. This site will be filled with information, how to's and tips for new creators.

  • Alex: The 13-year-old “gamer” using his love for video games to find a community online.
  • Veronica: The 18-year-old college student inspired to make beauty and lifestyle videos like her favorite Youtubers.
  • Kirk: The 30-year-old high school teacher who makes math tutorials looking to reach a large audience of students around the world.

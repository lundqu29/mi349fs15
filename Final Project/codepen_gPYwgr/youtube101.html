Making a Channel!

Making a YouTube account may seem like a minor step however, it is one of the most important steps! Here are a few things to think about when making your channel.

  • Your Email: All YouTube channels are linked with a Gmail account. If you already have a gmail you can sign in and create a channel with your existing email.

  • Your Channel Name: This is likely the most important step when making a channel. This is the name that will be used to represent your channel. Refer to "Branding Yourself" for tips on deciding your name!

Making a Schedule!

Something to think about when you are first starting off is, how does YouTube fit into my life? Is it just an occassional hobby or something you want to do more professionally? If your goal is to gain an audience having a regular schedule can be essential!

Dependibility is key. Think about your favorite T.V. show. It likely airs once a week at a certain time. Even though your audience can access your videos at whatever time, give them that same dependibility. Let's say you upload every Tuesday, that is something your audience can remember and look forward too.
